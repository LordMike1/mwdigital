Dartmouth Royal Regatta

Appreciating heritage and supporitng the community

Dartmouth Royal Regatta, a community-run non-profit event held in Dartmouth, Devon, boasts a rich history and royal patronage. As one of the oldest regattas in the UK, it attracts a diverse audience, including local rowing clubs, families, and tourists from across the country. The event's digital presence needed to reflect its prestigious heritage and broad appeal.


Project Objectives

In preparation for its 180th installment, the Dartmouth Royal Regatta identified the need for a comprehensive website redesign. The primary goal was to create a modern, user-friendly website that would enhance the digital marketing efforts and provide a seamless experience for visitors.


Challenges Faced

The existing website had accumulated a vast amount of information over time, resulting in a cluttered and confusing user experience. The main challenge was to reorganise this information to create a smoother user flow and make it easier for visitors to find the content they were searching for.


Scope of Work

Our agency provided a range of services to address these challenges, including:

    - Social media marketing analysis
    - Gradual website updates
    - A complete website redesign for 2025

Design and Development Process

We employed a data-driven and flexible approach throughout the design and development process. This included:

    - Analysing social media marketing reports to identify the most-searched-for information
    - Familiarising ourselves with the Regatta's operations to set impactful goals for the new website
    - Adapting existing branding to embody specified characteristics


Key Features and Functionality

The new website features several key functionalities designed to enhance user experience:

    - Content-Forward Design: Prioritising the display of relevant information
    - Call-to-Actions for Donations: Making it easy for visitors to support the event
    - Links to Social Media Channels: Promoting engagement across platforms
    - Accessibility Improvements: Ensuring the site is usable for all visitors


Technologies and Tools

To develop the new website, we utilised a range of technologies and tools, including:

    - Google Analytics: For tracking and analysing website performance
    - Semrush: For SEO optimisation and competitor analysis
    - Figma: For designing and prototyping the website
    - WordPress: As the content management system


Client Feedback

The Dartmouth Royal Regatta team was highly satisfied with the initial stages of the new website. They particularly appreciated the easily accessible information, clear user flow, and improved copywriting. These enhancements were crucial for meeting the needs of their diverse audience.


Results and Outcomes

As the website is still under development, measurable results are yet to be fully realised. However, the feedback from the client and initial user testing indicates a significant improvement in user experience and content accessibility.


Future Plans

Looking ahead, our agency will continue to support the Dartmouth Royal Regatta with ongoing digital marketing strategies. This includes a full website redevelopment for 2025 and further adaptations to the branding to ensure it remains relevant and engaging.


Conclusion

The collaboration between Dartmouth Royal Regatta and our agency has laid a strong foundation for a successful digital transformation. The new website is set to play a pivotal role in enhancing the event's online presence, ensuring that the Regatta continues to attract and engage a wide audience as it celebrates its 180th year. This case study demonstrates our commitment to delivering tailored, effective solutions that drive real results.
