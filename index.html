Digital Presence

How managing your socials works

It's only a suggestion on what might work but most projects play out this way

Step 1 - Let's Chat

We talk through what your business does online and what you would like help with. We then agree on a time frame and iron out the details of a contract.

Step 2 - Access

Just before work is set to begin we'll set up access to existing accounts or create new ones. Then on an agreed date, I'll start working on your socials!

Step 3 - Reports

Once a month I'll prepare a report on how things are going and go through it with you. We then talk through any plans you have for the next few months so we're in-sync.

Step 4 - Feedback

That's it! Of course this process isn't set in stone so it'll change to suit your needs, but once we we get going it'll repeat until we come to the end of our contract. At that point we'll go through an end-of-contract report and agree on the next steps.
