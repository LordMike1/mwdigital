Dartmouth and District Chamber of Commerce

Championing local businesses and improving the economy

The Dartmouth and District Chamber of Commerce, a newly established organisation, is dedicated to advocating for, promoting, and collaborating with local businesses to enhance the local economy. The Chamber's target audience includes business owners in Dartmouth and the surrounding areas, aiming to foster a supportive and prosperous business community.


Project Objectives

The primary goals of the business operational support project were to successfully launch the Chamber, promote it within the local community, analyse marketing efforts, and support the team in creating more efficient processes.


Challenges Faced

As a newly formed organisation, the Chamber faced the challenge of building its operations from scratch. This involved reactive working to adapt to changing priorities and tight deadlines. Additionally, the Chamber needed to unify a diverse range of businesses within a tight-knit community, each with specific concerns.


Scope of Work

Our agency provided a comprehensive range of services to support the Chamber's operational needs:

    - Digital presence analysis
    - Implementation of a full membership invoicing solution
    - Creation of a members directory on the website
    - Various operational improvements to support swift growth

Design and Development Process

We utilised a range of industry-standard tools and methodologies to deliver cost-effective solutions quickly, adapting them to the Chamber's evolving priorities and processes. This included using Excel for data management, WordPress for the website, and various social media marketing tools to enhance digital outreach.


Key Features and Functionality

Several key improvements and solutions were implemented during the project:

    - In-House Invoicing Solution: A comprehensive invoicing system that developed existing solutions to keep pace with the Chamber's rapid growth.
    - Members Database: Creation and maintenance of an extensive and searchable members database.
    - Performance Analysis Reports: Regular analysis reports of the Chamber's social media marketing efforts to track progress and identify areas for improvement.


Technologies and Tools

The following technologies and tools were utilised to provide operational support:

    - Excel: For data management and invoicing
    - WordPress: For website development and the members directory.
    - Social Media Marketing Tools: For enhancing digital presence and engagement.


Client Feedback

The Dartmouth and District Chamber of Commerce responded positively to the support provided. The Chamber grew to over 100 members within a few months of its launch and maintained an attendance of around 40 people at meetings. Successful campaigns were also completed to influence local decision-makers, such as improving town-centre parking.


Results and Outcomes

The Chamber experienced significant growth throughout the project, benefiting from the implemented solutions and support. Although ad-hoc support continues, the Chamber has established a strong foundation for its operations and continues to thrive.


Future Plans

While MW Digital no longer provides as much support, ad-hoc assistance continues to ensure the Chamber's ongoing success. The focus remains on sustaining growth and adapting to the evolving needs of the local business community.


Conclusion

The collaboration between the Dartmouth and District Chamber of Commerce and our agency has resulted in a robust operational framework that supports the Chamber's mission to enhance the local economy. This case study highlights our commitment to delivering tailored solutions that drive tangible results, ensuring the Chamber's continued growth and success in the business community.
